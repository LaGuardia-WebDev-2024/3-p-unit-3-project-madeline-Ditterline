Elven Bread

  • Time: 208-250 minutes
  • Serves: Those who desire it
Ingredients Quantity
Active dry yeast 1/2 ounce
Lukewarm whole milk 1 1/2 cups
Honey 1/2 cup
Beaten Eggs 3
Melted unsalted butter 1/4 cup
Kosher salt 2 Teaspoons
Whole Wheat Flour 1 cup
All Purpose Flour 5+ cups
Sugar 1/2 cup
All Purpose flour (again) 1 tablespoon
Ground Cinnamon 1 tablespoon

Step 1: Dissolve the yeast in 1/4 cup of milk.

Step 2: Add the remaining milk, two eggs, the honey, the butter, salt, the whole wheat flour, and 5 cups of the all-purpose flour.

Step 3: Mix until well combined.

Step 4: Knead the dough from 8-15 minutes, and transfer it into a large bowl (lightly greased). Cover it with a clean kitchen towel and leave to rise for an hour.

Step 5: While the dough is rising, combine the sugar, 1 tablespoon of all purpose flour, and Cinnamon in a bowl. Beat the remaining egg in a separate bowl with water to make egg wash.

Step 6: Butter two 8.5 x 4.5 inch loaf pans and cut the dough in half.

Step 7: Roll each half of the dough into a 6 x 20 rectangle and brush the dough with the egg wash.

Step 8: Sprinkle the cinnamon/sugar /flour mixture over the egg wash.

Step 9: Roll the dough, starting from the short side, into a log and place it in the pan.

Step 10: Cover the pans and leave the dough to rise for about 1.5-2 hours.

Step 11: Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.

Step 12: Bake for 10 minutes, then lower the oven temperature to 350 and bake for another 25-30 minutes.

Drow Mushroom Steaks

  • Serves 6

Ingredients Quantity
Olive Oil Three tablespoons
Fresh Lemon Juice 2 tablespoons
Balsamic Vinegar 2 tablespoons
Fresh thyme leaves 1 tablespoon
Kosher Salt 1/2 teaspoon
freshly ground black pepper However much you want
Large Portobello mushroom caps Six

Step 1:Whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, balsamic vinegar, thyme, salt, and pepper in a large, shallow dish.

Step 2: Put the mushrooms in the dish and allow them to marinate for 15 minutes, flipping them over halfway through.

Step 3: Heat up a grill (or a grill pan) to medium high heat and grill the mushrooms for 10-12 minutes, flipping them over halfway through.

Black Pudding

  • Serves 4
Ingredients Quantity
Light brown sugar 2/3 cup, packed
Instant Espresso Powder 1/4 cup
Dutch Cocoa Powder 3 tablespoons
Cornstarch 3 tablespoons
Kosher Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Egg Yolks Three
Heavy cream 3/4 cup
Unsalted butter 1/4 cup
Pure Vanilla Extract 1.5 teaspoons
Coffee Liqueur 1 tablespoon
Brandy 2 teaspoons
Chocolate shavings To taste (for serving)

Step 1: In a large saucepan, whisk together the brown sugar, espresso powder, cocoa powder, cornstarch, and salt.

Step 2: Add the egg yolks and cream and whisk until the mixture is smooth.

Step 3: Set the pan over medium heat and cook, whisking consistently as you do so for about 8-10 minutes.

Step 4: Remove the pan from the heat and add the butter, vanilla, coffee liqueur, and brandy. Whisk until the butter is fully incorporated.

Step 5: Put a strainer over a clean bowl and pour in the pudding, then use a flexible spatula to gently work the pudding through the strainer.

Step 6: Press a piece of parchment paper directly onto the surface of the pudding and put the pudding in the refrigerator until it is set.

Melted Cheeses With Chunky Tomato Broth

  • Serves 4
Ingredients Quantity
salted butter 2 tablespoons
Chopped large onion one
Bay leaf One
kosher salt to taste
Light brown sugar 1 tablespoon
tomato paste 1 tablespoon
Low-sodium chicken broth 4 cups
white rice 2 tablespoons
peeled whole tomatoes 1 can
fresh black pepper to taste
Softened, salted butter 6 tablespoons
Sandwich bread, 1/2 inch 8 slices
Coarsely grated firm cheese 8 ounces

Step 1: Over medium heat, melt 2 tablespoons of butter in a large saucepan.

Step 2: Add the onion, bay leaf, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt and cook for about 5 minutes.

Step 3: Add the brown sugar and tomato paste and cook for about 1 minute.

Step 4: Add the chicken broth and rice, and all but three of the tomatoes in the can.

Step 5: Simmer the mixture for about 20 minutes.

Step 6: Puree the mixture until it is smooth.

Step 7: Chop the reserved tomatoes and put them in the soup. Stir.

Step 8: Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread, then place them on the cutting board buttered sides down.

Step 9: put about 1/2 cup shredded cheese on four slices, and more butter on the other four slices.

Step 10: It's grilled cheese bro. It's grilled cheese. Just make grilled cheese. Serve the grilled cheese and the soup together.
