Recipe #1

  • Time: 208-250 minutes
  • Serves: Those who desire it
Ingredients Quantity
Active dry yeast 1/2 ounce
Lukewarm whole milk 1 1/2 cups
Honey 1/2 cup
Beaten Eggs 3
Melted unsalted butter 1/4 cup
Kosher salt 2 Teaspoons
Whole Wheat Flour 1 cup
All Purpose Flour 5+ cups
Sugar 1/2 cup
All Purpose flour (again) 1 tablespoon
Ground Cinnamon 1 tablespoon

Step 1: Dissolve the yeast in 1/4 cup of milk.

Step 2: Add the remaining milk, two eggs, the honey, the butter, salt, the whole wheat flour, and 5 cups of the all-purpose flour.

Step 3: Mix until well combined.

Step 4: Knead the dough from 8-15 minutes, and transfer it into a large bowl (lightly greased). Cover it with a clean kitchen towel and leave to rise for an hour.

Step 5: While the dough is rising, combine the sugar, 1 tablespoon of all purpose flour, and Cinnamon in a bowl. Beat the remaining egg in a separate bowl with water to make egg wash.

Step 6: Butter two 8.5 x 4.5 inch loaf pans and cut the dough in half.
